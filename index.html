Arun Srivatsan Rangaprasad

I graduated in May 2018 with a Ph.D. from Biorobotics Laboratory in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. I was advised by Howie Choset.

For my thesis, I developed probabilistic approaches for pose estimation.
